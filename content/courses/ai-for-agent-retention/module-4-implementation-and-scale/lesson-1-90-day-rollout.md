# Your 90-Day Rollout Plan

Everything in the first three modules is easy to understand and hard to implement while also running a full agency. This lesson gives you a 90-day plan that respects reality.

## Days 1 to 14: Setup
Block two hours on your calendar. Not two hours this month. Two hours this week. In those two hours you do exactly three things.

One, export your next 90 days of upcoming renewals from your AMS into a spreadsheet. Columns: client name, policy type, renewal date, premium, any notes.

Two, set up the Google Sheet dashboard from Module 3. Five rows, five values, one weekly refresh ritual.

Three, pick one AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, or whatever you prefer) and save the personalization prompt from Module 2 as a template you can reuse in 10 seconds.

That is the entire setup phase. Two hours. No tools to buy, no integrations to build.

## Days 15 to 30: First sequence
In week three, pick ten clients from your upcoming renewals and run the six-touch sequence manually. Yes, manually. You are not building a Zap yet. You are proving to yourself that the sequence works and that your voice sounds right.

For each of the ten, use the AI prompt to draft each touch on the day it should go out. Approve, edit for 30 seconds, send. Track response rates in your dashboard.

By the end of week four you will have sent about 30 total touches (three per client times ten clients) and you should be seeing response rates above 30 percent. If not, your voice samples or client facts need work. Don't move on until that number looks right.

## Days 31 to 60: Layer the dashboard
In weeks five through eight, start running the Monday dashboard ritual. Every Monday morning, 30 minutes, fill in the five metrics. By the end of week eight you should be automatically identifying your top save candidates each week without thinking about it.

Also in this phase, start layering in the second channel (SMS) from Lesson 2.2. Pick the five nudge moments where a text is appropriate, and start actually sending them.

## Days 61 to 90: Scale and measure
In weeks nine through twelve, you scale the sequence from ten clients to your full upcoming renewal pipeline. This is the moment where the AI workflow becomes essential. Trying to draft 60 personalized touches a week without a template and a tool is brutal. With both, it takes 30 minutes.

At day 90, go back to your baseline number from Module 1 and recalculate. How many of your 90-day renewals actually retained? What was your lapse rate in that window? Compare it to the lapse rate from the same 90-day window last year.

## What not to do in 90 days
Don't migrate AMS platforms. Don't build custom software. Don't hire anyone. Don't buy new tools beyond the AI subscription you probably already have. Don't try to automate the human-touch calls.

The 90-day goal is not a finished, perfect system. The goal is proof that the system works so you can justify investing more time in the next 90 days.

**Do this today:** Put the two-hour setup block on your calendar this week. If it is not on the calendar, it will not happen.

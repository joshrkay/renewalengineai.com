# AI Agency Operations Bootcamp

## Who this is for
Agency owners and operators who want AI working across the whole business, not just in one corner. If AI for Agent Retention was a scalpel, this bootcamp is the full operating room. You should take this course if you have at least one employee beyond yourself, you have started thinking about SOPs and playbooks, and you believe the next version of your agency is mostly automated.

## What you'll build
Over five modules and fifteen lessons you will build working AI systems across the five operational zones of an independent agency: foundations, intake, quoting and underwriting, client service, and ops playbooks. By the end you will have running workflows that handle new lead capture, quote comparison, document extraction, client service triage, and ongoing SOP maintenance.

This is not a theoretical course. Every lesson produces an artifact you can drop into your agency this week.

## Format and pricing
Two delivery options.

**Self-paced Premium ($797)**: Video lessons, written content, all templates, all prompts, private community access, lifetime updates. Work through it on your own timeline.

**Live Cohort ($1,297)**: Everything in self-paced, plus eight weekly live calls with the instructor and other agency owners, peer review of your workflows, and direct feedback on your implementations. Cohorts run quarterly with 15 seats each.

## Prerequisites
Comfort with basic spreadsheet work, an active AI subscription (ChatGPT Plus, Claude, or similar), and enough time to spend four to six hours per week on course work during the first month. You do not need any programming background.

## Guarantee
Thirty days, no questions. Build one workflow from the course. If it doesn't save you real hours in the first month, we refund the full cost.

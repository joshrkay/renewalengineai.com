### Variant A Elements That Drove Improvement

1. **Headline**: "Discover How Top Agents Save 15+ Hours Weekly"
   • More benefit-driven and specific vs generic "Get Started with AI Automation"
   • Clear value proposition resonates with target audience pain points

2. **CTA Button Text**: "Get My Free Automation Guide"
   • More personal and action-oriented vs passive "Get Free Access to Lead Magnet"
   • "My" creates sense of ownership and immediate benefit

3. **CTA Button Color**: Green (#10b981) vs Blue (#3b82f6)
   • Green associated with 'go', 'yes', 'approval' and growth
   • Better visual contrast against form background

4. **Description**: "Get instant access to: 5 AI Automations..."
   • More immediate and direct vs "We'll send you our free guide..."
   • Sets expectation of immediate value delivery

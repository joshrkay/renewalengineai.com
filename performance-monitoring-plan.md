## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to Monitor

### 1. Funnel Conversion Metrics
   • Lead Capture Rate: (Form submissions / Page visitors) × 100
   • Lead Quality Score: Based on follow-up engagement and conversion potential
   • Cost Per Lead (CPL): Total marketing spend ÷ Number of leads
   • Lead to Customer Conversion Rate: (Customers / Leads) × 100

### 2. Engagement Metrics
   • Page View Duration: Average time spent on key pages
   • Bounce Rate: (Single page visits / Total entries) × 100
   • Click Through Rate (CTR): (Clicks / Impressions) × 100
   • Form Initiation Rate: (Form interactions / Page visitors) × 100

### 3. Revenue Metrics
   • Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Growth
   • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Total acquisition spend ÷ New customers
   • Lifetime Value (LTV): Average revenue per customer × Average lifespan
   • LTV to CAC Ratio: LTV ÷ CAC

### 4. Campaign Performance Metrics
   • Email Open Rate: (Opened emails / Sent emails) × 100
   • Email Click Through Rate: (Clicked emails / Sent emails) × 100
   • Email Conversion Rate: (Converted emails / Sent emails) × 100
   • Social Media Engagement Rate: (Engagements / Impressions) × 100
   • Social Media Click Through Rate: (Link clicks / Impressions) × 100

## Monitoring Tools and Frequency

### Real-Time Monitoring (Hourly/Daily)
   • Google Analytics Real-Time: Active users, page views, events
   • Google Analytics Events: Form submissions, lead captures, CTA clicks
   • Email Service Provider: Sent, delivered, opened, clicked metrics
   • Social Media Platforms: Impressions, engagements, link clicks

### Daily Reporting (Every 24 Hours)
   • Daily lead count and quality assessment
   • Daily email campaign performance
   • Daily social media performance
   • Daily funnel conversion rates

### Weekly Analysis (Every 7 Days)
   • Week-over-week trend analysis
   • A/B test result validation
   • Funnel stage conversion analysis
   • Campaign ROI calculation

### Monthly Deep Dive (Monthly)
   • Month-over-month performance comparison
   • Cohort analysis (lead quality over time)
   • Customer lifetime value calculation
   • Channel effectiveness ranking

## Alert Thresholds and Responses

### Positive Triggers (Investigate and Scale)
   • Conversion rate increase ≥15% vs baseline: Scale winning elements
   • Lead quality improvement ≥10%: Increase investment in channel
   • CPL decrease ≥20%: Allocate more budget to successful tactics
   • Engagement rate increase ≥25%: Test similar creative approaches

### Negative Triggers (Investigate and Fix)
   • Conversion rate decrease ≥10%: Roll back recent changes
   • Bounce rate increase ≥15%: Investigate page relevance and loading speed
   • Form abandonment increase ≥20%: Simplify form and improve value proposition
   • Email open rate decrease ≥15%: Test new subject lines and sender reputation
